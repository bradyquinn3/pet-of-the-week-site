Support Pet of the Week

Pet of the Week started in high school as a way to celebrate pets and the joy they bring. It has grown because people enjoy seeing their pets featured. I continue this project in college simply because it matters to me and the community.

This is all about the pets and the people who love them. I never include my name because it is not about me. If you enjoy this project and want to contribute, any support is appreciated, but it is never required.
